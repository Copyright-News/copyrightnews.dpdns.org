Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate our publication and improve our content. This includes anonymous analytics data (page views, referrer URLs) and any information you voluntarily submit through our contact or story submission forms (name, email, pitch details).

2. How We Use Your Information

Submitted information is used solely for editorial review and communication. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third-party marketers or data brokers.

3. Cookies & Tracking

This site uses essential cookies for basic functionality and Cloudflare analytics. We do not use invasive cross-site tracking cookies or third-party advertising networks.

4. Third-Party Services

Our infrastructure is hosted via Cloudflare. Our editorial workflow utilizes GitHub. Both services maintain their own strict privacy and security policies regarding data processed through their networks.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to address your inquiry or review your pitch. If a story pitch is rejected, the data is deleted from our active repository.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact our editorial desk at admin@copyrightnews.dpdns.org.
